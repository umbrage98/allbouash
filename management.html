Enrollment Information

College offers the NEB Affiliated +2 program.

Students can choose the following combined subjects:

Grade XI Grade XII
Compulsory English Compulsory English
Compulsory Nepali Compulsory Nepali
Social Studies Social Studies

Economics Economics
Business Studies or Hotel Management or Computer Science or Business Maths Business Studies or Hotel Management or Computer Science or Business Maths

Enrollment Eligibility

Students with at least Grade C in the SEE examinations and 50% in the entrance Examinations.

Entrance Test Subjects and Weightage

  • English: 40%
  • Mathematics: 40%
  • GK / IQ: 20%

Enrollment Procedure

  1. The eligible students have to fill up the admission form and sit for the entrance test.
  2. As they get through the test, they have to sit for an interview with their parents.
  3. Then, they have to enroll themselves within three days submitting the following documents:
  • A copy of SEE Grade Sheet.
  • A copy of Character Certificate.
  • Two Passport size photos.

Note: The management will not be bound to take in the students after all the seats are occupied even though they have met the eligibility criteria.
