Who We Are

Nestled in the picturesque suburb of Dhapakhel, Lalitpur, Nepal, Allbouash School stands as a paragon of educational excellence. Since its establishment in 2064, the school has been steadfast in its commitment to nurturing the intellectual, creative, and physical prowess of its students.

Foundational Principles:

  • Inception: Began with a vision to create an inclusive and stimulating educational environment.
  • Growth: Expanded from initial offerings of Nursery to grade III to a full-fledged higher secondary school.
  • Development: Continuously evolving to meet the educational needs of a diverse student body.

Campus and Facilities:

  • Location: Strategically placed 1.5 kilometers from Sat Dobato point on the Ring road.
  • Infrastructure: Modern academic buildings with spacious classrooms and advanced laboratories.
  • Residential Options: Comfortable hostel buildings providing a secure and nurturing living space.
  • Administrative Support: Efficient administrative offices ensuring the smooth functioning of the school.
  • Nutrition: A welcoming cafeteria offering healthy and delicious meals to students and staff.
  • Sports: Expansive playing fields for football, basketball, and other sports activities.
  • Arts and Culture: Dedicated spaces for extracurricular pursuits like gymnastics, judo, and various musical instruments.

Academic Excellence:

  • Curriculum: A broad and balanced curriculum that fosters critical thinking and problem-solving skills.
  • Teaching Methodology: Interactive and student-centered teaching approaches.
  • Technology Integration: Smart classrooms equipped with the latest educational technology.

Extracurricular Activities:

  • Sports Programs: Facilities and coaching for a wide range of sports to promote teamwork and physical health.
  • Arts Education: Programs in visual and performing arts to encourage creativity and self-expression.
  • Music and Dance: Classes for various instruments and dance forms to cultivate artistic talents.

Community Engagement:

  • Service Projects: Initiatives that encourage students to engage with and contribute to the local community.
  • Cultural Events: Celebrations and events that highlight the rich cultural heritage of Nepal.

Future Aspirations:

  • Innovation: A commitment to incorporating cutting-edge teaching methods and technologies.
  • Global Perspective: Preparing students to be global citizens with a deep understanding of international issues.
  • Leadership Development: Programs designed to develop leadership skills and civic responsibility

Invitation to Prospective Students:

  • Open House: Opportunities for prospective students and families to visit and experience the school’s offerings.
  • Enrollment: A welcoming enrollment process that values diversity and individuality.
  • Supportive Environment: Continuously evolving to meet the educational needs of a diverse student body.

Our Mission

At Allbouash School, we are driven by a mission that is both ambitious and inspiring. Our purpose is to cultivate an environment where every student is empowered to explore, learn, and grow. We believe that education is the most powerful tool for personal development and societal transformation.

Our academic mission is to challenge each student to reach their highest potential. We provide a rigorous curriculum that is rich in content and offers depth and breadth across disciplines. Our educators are passionate about teaching and are committed to fostering a love for learning that lasts a lifetime.

We prioritize the development of critical thinking skills, encouraging our students to question, analyze, and synthesize information. Problem-solving is at the heart of our educational approach, preparing our students to navigate complex challenges with confidence and creativity.

At Allbouash School, we recognize that the future belongs to innovators and creators. Our programs are designed to inspire creativity, whether it’s through art, music, science, or technology. We provide the resources and support for students to turn their imaginative ideas into reality.

Our commitment to ethical education is unwavering. We strive to instill values of integrity, respect, and empathy. As global citizens, our students learn to appreciate cultural diversity and to act with a sense of responsibility towards the global community.

Leadership development is integral to our mission. We aim to nurture leaders who are not only academically accomplished but also emotionally intelligent and socially aware. Personal growth is fostered through a variety of experiences that build character and resilience.

We believe that a healthy body supports a healthy mind. Our physical education programs and sports activities promote fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship. Emotional well-being is supported through counseling services and a nurturing school environment.

Service to the community is a key aspect of our mission. We encourage our students to engage in service projects and to contribute positively to society. Through these experiences, they develop a sense of empathy and a commitment to making a difference.

Allbouash School is a place where diversity is celebrated and inclusivity is practiced. We welcome students from all backgrounds and create an environment where every individual feels valued and respected.

We prepare our students for a world that is constantly evolving. Our educational programs are forward-thinking and adaptive, ensuring that our students are equipped with the skills and knowledge to succeed in the future.

Our Vision

"In the pursuit of educational excellence, Allbouash School stands as a citadel of learning, where every child is cherished and every mind is challenged to surpass the ordinary. Our vision is to cultivate a sanctuary of education that thrives on the principles of innovation, inclusivity, and integrity.

We envision Allbouash School as a nurturing ground for the leaders of tomorrow, where students are not only taught but inspired to learn, question, and create. We aim to foster a culture of academic rigor coupled with a compassionate understanding of the diverse world we inhabit.

Our school is committed to providing an environment that supports the intellectual, artistic, and personal growth of our students. We believe in the power of education to transform lives and the importance of adapting to the evolving demands of the global landscape.

At Allbouash School, we strive to:

  • - Develop critical and creative thinking skills that empower students to solve complex problems.
  • - Encourage a spirit of inquiry and a love for lifelong learning.
  • - Promote a sense of social responsibility and ethical leadership.
  • - Cultivate an appreciation for the arts, culture, and humanities.
  • - physical health and well-being through comprehensive sports and wellness programs.
  • - emotional intelligence and resilience to prepare students for the challenges of life.
  • - a community where every individual is valued and diversity is celebrated.
  • - technology and innovation into the learning process to prepare students for the digital age.
  • - with local and global communities to broaden perspectives and encourage active citizenship.
  • - environmental consciousness and a commitment to sustainable living practices.

Our vision extends to creating a collaborative partnership between educators, families, and the community, ensuring that every student's journey at Allbouash School is enriching and transformative. We are dedicated to sculpting a future where our students are not only successful in their careers but also contribute meaningfully to society.

Allbouash School is more than an institution; it is a movement towards a brighter, more enlightened future. We stand firm in our belief that education is the cornerstone of progress and the most powerful tool we can offer our youth. As we look ahead, we remain unwavering in our mission to light the way for generations to come, guiding them towards a horizon filled with promise, opportunity, and hope."
