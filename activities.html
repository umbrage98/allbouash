Activities

Extra Curricular & Co-curricular Activities

MOLIHSS conducts various co-curricular and extracurricular activities to nurture students' talents in different areas of interest. The programs and dates are mentioned on the calendar.

Students’ Council

Instilling leadership qualities in the students is the need of the hour. MOLIHSS has a Student’s Council consisting of a team of students elected from each class. The Student’s Council assists the management in the smooth running of the classes and various co-curricular-cum-extra-curricular activities.

Clubs

The students can also join the following clubs to make use of their free time and refine their skills:

  • Sports Club
  • Music Club
  • Dance Club
  • Dramatics Club
  • Social Service Club

House Division

In order to foster a sense of competitiveness and group work, MOLIHSS divides the students into four Houses which, in turn, compete in the activities conducted in or outside the institution.
